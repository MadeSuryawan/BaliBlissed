Frequently Asked Questions

Which routes are included in this service?

This service covers most of Bali, including Ubud City Centre. Areas in North Bali beyond Ubud may incur an additional surcharge depending on your chosen package. You can customize your route and starting point by listing your preferred itinerary during checkout.

How long can I stay at each attraction?

The duration at each location is entirely up to you within your booked hours. Your driver will accommodate your preferences, though they may offer suggestions to help you maximize your time.

What's included in the fees, and what are the extra charges?

The fee covers your private vehicle, driver, and fuel costs. Not included are entrance tickets to attractions, meals (including the driver's meal for bookings over 6 hours), and surcharges for certain northern destinations.

When will I receive my driver and vehicle information?

You'll receive your driver's contact information and vehicle details one day before your scheduled service via email or WhatsApp.

Can I be picked up from the airport?

Yes, airport pickup is available at no additional cost. Your driver will wait for up to 120 minutes after your flight's arrival, holding a sign with the lead traveler's name.

What happens if I need to extend my rental hours?

If you wish to extend your rental hours on the day of service, you can discuss this directly with your driver. Additional hours are charged at IDR 100,000 per hour, payable directly to the driver.

Is there a luggage limit for airport pickups?

Standard luggage size is 61cm; larger bags count as 2 pieces and take up passenger space. Please specify your luggage requirements when booking to ensure an appropriately sized vehicle.
