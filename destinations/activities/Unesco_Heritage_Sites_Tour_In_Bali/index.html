Bali's Cultural & Natural Jewels: UNESCO Heritage Tour

Explore iconic temples like Taman Ayun, Ulun Danu Beratan, and Tanah Lot, plus the breathtaking Jatiluwih Rice Terraces.

Journey Through Bali's Heritage

Locations

Jatiluwih

Duration

Full Day (approx. 8-10 hours)

Best Experience

Daytime (Sunset at Tanah Lot)

Immerse yourself in the rich culture and stunning natural beauty of Bali on this comprehensive tour visiting some of the island's most significant landmarks, including a UNESCO World Heritage site. Your journey begins at the magnificent Taman Ayun Temple in Mengwi, the former Royal Temple, renowned for its impressive traditional Balinese architecture, serene moats, and expansive, beautifully manicured gardens. It's a perfect introduction to Bali's spiritual heart.

Next, venture north into the cool highlands to Bedugul, where the iconic Ulun Danu Beratan Temple awaits. Seemingly floating on the calm waters of Lake Bratan, this picturesque temple dedicated to the lake goddess Dewi Danu offers truly breathtaking photo opportunities against a backdrop of misty mountains. Feel the tranquility of this sacred site.

Continue your exploration to the awe-inspiring Jatiluwih Rice Terraces, a UNESCO World Heritage site recognized for its stunningly beautiful, contoured landscapes and the traditional 'subak' irrigation system. Wander through the vast expanse of emerald-green paddies and appreciate the harmonious relationship between nature and Balinese farming culture. Finally, cap off your cultural journey with a visit to the legendary Tanah Lot Temple. Perched dramatically on a rock formation amidst crashing waves, this sea temple is one of Bali's most photographed icons, especially magical during sunset.
