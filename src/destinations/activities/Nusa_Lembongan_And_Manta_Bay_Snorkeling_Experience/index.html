Just a 30-minute speedboat ride from Bali lies a snorkeler's paradise waiting to be discovered. Nusa Lembongan and its neighboring Nusa Penida are home to some of Indonesia's most spectacular marine ecosystems, where vibrant coral gardens teem with tropical fish and the legendary Manta Bay offers unforgettable encounters with graceful ocean giants. Our expertly guided snorkeling adventure takes you to the very best spots around these pristine islands, providing an underwater experience that rivals the world's top diving destinations—all without needing scuba certification.

The journey begins as you board our modern speedboat at Sanur Harbor, skimming across the dazzling blue Bali Sea toward the island silhouettes on the horizon. Upon arrival at Nusa Lembongan, you'll be greeted by the sight of crystal-clear waters in shades of turquoise and aquamarine that seem almost unreal. Your experienced English-speaking guide will conduct a brief safety orientation before providing you with high-quality snorkeling equipment perfectly suited to exploring these waters. Then it's time to slip beneath the surface at your first snorkeling spot, where an underwater wonderland of colorful coral formations and darting reef fish awaits.

The highlight of this adventure is undoubtedly Manta Bay, a world-famous site where magnificent manta rays with wingspans of up to five meters gracefully glide through the water. Unlike many marine encounters that require luck and timing, manta sightings here are remarkably consistent year-round, making this one of the few places on earth where you can reliably swim alongside these gentle ocean giants. As you float at the surface, watch in awe as these majestic creatures perform their underwater ballet, sometimes coming close enough that you can appreciate every detail of their elegant movements. For those who choose the full-day option, the adventure continues with a visit to Nusa Ceningan's iconic Blue Lagoon and Dream Beach, where you'll have time to relax on powdery white sand and capture Instagram-worthy photos of the dramatic coastal scenery.
