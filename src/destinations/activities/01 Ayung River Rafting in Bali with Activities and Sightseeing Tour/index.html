Nestled within Bali's lush heartland, the Ayung River cuts a magnificent path through verdant rainforests, dramatic gorges, and past hidden waterfalls. Our signature rafting adventure invites you to experience the island's natural splendor from a unique perspective—aboard a sturdy raft navigating the gentle rapids of Bali's longest and most scenic river. This isn't just any rafting trip; it's a journey through a living gallery of Bali's most spectacular landscapes, where each bend in the river reveals new wonders that can't be accessed by road.

Your adventure begins with a convenient pickup from your accommodation, followed by a scenic drive through traditional villages and terraced landscapes to our private river launch point. Here, our professional guides—all certified in swift water rescue and first aid—will equip you with top-quality gear and provide a comprehensive safety briefing. Then, the real excitement begins as you navigate approximately 10 kilometers of Class II and III rapids, perfect for both first-timers and experienced rafters. Feel the refreshing splash of crystal-clear water as your expert guide helps you maneuver through thrilling rapids while pointing out hidden waterfalls, ancient stone carvings, and exotic wildlife along the riverbanks.

After approximately two hours of paddling adventure, you'll arrive at our exclusive riverside facility where a hot shower and a change of clothes await. Celebrate your accomplishment with a sumptuous Indonesian buffet lunch served in an open-air pavilion overlooking the river you've just conquered. For those seeking to enhance their Bali experience, our premium package includes an afternoon visit to the stunning Kanto Lampo and Tibumana Waterfalls—two of Bali's most photogenic cascades where you can swim in natural pools beneath falling water. Alternatively, combine your rafting with an exhilarating ATV ride through rice fields and jungle paths, or visit cultural highlights including the sacred Monkey Forest and the iconic Tegalalang Rice Terrace. Whatever option you choose, this day promises to be a highlight of your Bali adventure—combining natural beauty, physical activity, and cultural immersion in perfect balance.
