Beach Horse Riding Experience at Seminyak Beach

Feel the ocean breeze and the rhythm of hooves on Bali's iconic black sand beach.

Ride Along Seminyak Beach

Location

Seminyak Beach

View on Google map

Price

From $50 / person

Best Time to Ride

Morning or Late Afternoon (Sunset)

Imagine the gentle rhythm of waves accompanying the steady clip-clop of hooves as you ride along the stunning black sands of Seminyak Beach. This isn't just horse riding; it's an unforgettable Bali experience, blending adventure with the serene beauty of the coastline. Whether you're a seasoned equestrian or have never sat on a horse before, this activity is designed for everyone.

Our professional and friendly guides ensure your safety and comfort, providing all necessary equipment and instruction. Feel the unique connection with your horse as you meander along the shore, the ocean breeze in your hair and the vast expanse of the Indian Ocean stretching before you. It's a perfect activity to share with loved ones, creating memories that will last a lifetime.

Choose a morning session to enjoy the beach at its most peaceful, avoiding the midday crowds. Or, opt for a magical sunset ride, where the sky transforms into a breathtaking canvas of oranges, pinks, and purples, reflecting off the wet sand – a truly cinematic moment captured from a unique vantage point.

Tour Highlights

  • Create lasting memories riding horses on iconic Seminyak Beach.
  • Experience the unique sensation of riding on beautiful black sand.
  • Perfect for couples, families, and solo adventurers.
  • Choose a tranquil morning ride or a spectacular sunset experience.
  • Beginner-friendly with expert guides ensuring safety.
  • Enjoy stunning coastal views and the refreshing sea breeze.

What's Included

  • Horse riding experience (approx. 1-1.5 hours)
  • Experienced and trained guides
  • All necessary safety equipment (helmets, etc.)
  • Basic riding instruction
  • Bottled water
  • Insurance coverage

Tip: Wear comfortable long pants or shorts and closed-toe shoes. Don't forget sunscreen and sunglasses, especially for daytime rides. Booking in advance is highly recommended, particularly for sunset slots. Remember your camera!

Essential Experiences

  • Feel the thrill and freedom of riding along the shoreline.
  • Witness the stunning contrast of the black sand and blue ocean.
  • Capture incredible photos, especially during the golden hour at sunset.
  • Connect with gentle, well-cared-for horses.
  • Enjoy a peaceful escape from the hustle and bustle.
  • Learn basic horse handling from friendly local guides.
  • Create a unique Bali memory unlike any other.
