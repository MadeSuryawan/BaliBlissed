Imagine floating weightlessly through crystal-clear waters, surrounded by vibrant coral gardens and colorful fish, while exploring one of the world's most accessible shipwrecks. Welcome to Tulamben, home to the legendary USAT Liberty wreck—a World War II cargo ship transformed into a thriving underwater ecosystem that offers an unparalleled diving experience for beginners and experienced divers alike. Our Discover Scuba Day Trip provides the perfect opportunity for non-divers to experience the magic of breathing underwater in one of Bali's premier dive destinations.

Your adventure begins with a comprehensive pool session led by certified PADI instructors who prioritize both safety and fun. In the controlled environment of a swimming pool in either Sanur or Tulamben, you'll learn essential diving skills and gain confidence before venturing into the open ocean. Feel the initial thrill of breathing underwater and experience the sensation of weightlessness that makes diving so addictive. Our patient instructors ensure you're comfortable with the equipment and basic techniques before moving to the next stage of your journey.

After mastering the fundamentals, you'll embark on two guided ocean dives in the stunning waters of Tulamben. The first dive typically explores the shallow coral gardens, where you'll encounter a kaleidoscope of marine life—from tiny, inquisitive clownfish darting among anemones to graceful sea turtles gliding through the water. Your second dive often includes parts of the USAT Liberty wreck, where you'll witness how nature has transformed this historic vessel into a living artificial reef teeming with marine biodiversity. Throughout both dives, your instructor remains by your side, ensuring your safety while helping you spot fascinating underwater creatures you might otherwise miss.

This Discover Scuba experience requires no prior diving knowledge and is specifically designed for beginners aged 10 and above. It's the perfect way to sample the underwater world without committing to a full certification course. Many participants find themselves so captivated by the experience that they continue their diving journey with additional courses. Whether you're seeking a unique adventure during your Bali vacation or considering diving as a new hobby, our Tulamben Discover Scuba Day Trip offers an unforgettable introduction to the wonders beneath the waves.
