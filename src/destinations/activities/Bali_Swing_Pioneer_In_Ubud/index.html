Soar Above the Jungle: Bali Swing Pioneer

Experience the thrill of Ubud's iconic swings and capture breathtaking photos against a stunning natural backdrop.

Swing into Paradise

Location

Bali Swing, Bongkasa

View on Google map

Opening Hours

Daily: 08:00 - 17:00

Best Time to Visit

Morning (to avoid queues)

No trip to Ubud is truly complete without experiencing the exhilaration of the famous Bali Swing! Prepare for an adrenaline rush like no other as you soar high above the lush jungle canopy, with breathtaking views of the river valley and cascading waterfalls stretching out before you. It's more than just a swing; it's a chance to feel truly free, surrounded by the incredible natural beauty of Bali.

Choose from a variety of swings, ranging in height from a gentle 10 meters (33 feet) to a heart-pounding 78 meters (256 feet) above the ground! Whether you ride solo or share the moment on a tandem swing, the feeling of gliding through the air against such an epic backdrop is unforgettable. Beyond the swings, the Bali Swing Pioneer area is an Instagrammer's dream, filled with unique photo opportunities.

Pose in whimsical bird nests perched high above the valley, capture romantic shots on the heart-shaped rock, or simply relax on the sky bed and soak in the panoramic vistas. Even if swinging isn't your style, the entrance package allows you to enjoy the stunning location and capture those perfect Bali photos.

Swing Active Package Highlights

  • Experience multiple swings (2 Single, 1 Romantic, 1 Bed Swing).
  • Access unique photo spots like the Sky Bed, Chicken Nest, and Bird Nest.
  • Enjoy stunning views of the jungle, canyon, and river waterfall.
  • Create unforgettable memories and envy-inducing photos.
  • Insurance provided by the operator for peace of mind.
  • Optional round-trip hotel transfers available for convenience.

Important Information & Tips

  • Weight limit for all swings: 200 kg (single or tandem).
  • Activity not recommended for pregnant women, seniors, or those with certain medical conditions (e.g., high blood pressure, epilepsy).
  • Not suitable for persons with impaired physical mobility or wheelchairs.
  • Infants (0-2) are not recommended to ride the swings for safety.
  • Outside food and drinks are not permitted.
  • Some activities might be temporarily unavailable due to maintenance or safety reasons.

Insider Tips:

  • Visit early in the morning (around opening time) to avoid the longest queues.
  • If one swing has a queue, try others first!
  • Bring: Camera, comfortable clothing (great for photos!), hat, sunglasses, sunblock, cash for extras.
  • Consider bringing a change of clothes if participating in any nearby water activities.
  • Download WhatsApp for easy communication with local operators.

  • Pose in unique photo spots like giant bird nests.
  • Experience a different perspective of Bali's natural beauty.
  • Share a romantic moment on the tandem swing.
  • Overcome your fear of heights in a safe and beautiful environment.
