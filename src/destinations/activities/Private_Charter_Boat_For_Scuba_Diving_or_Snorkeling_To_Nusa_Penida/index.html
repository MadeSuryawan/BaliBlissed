Escape the crowds and experience the marine wonders of Nusa Penida on your own terms with our exclusive private boat charter. Just a 45-minute boat ride from Bali's mainland, Nusa Penida offers some of Indonesia's most spectacular underwater landscapes and marine biodiversity. Our private charter service gives you the freedom to design your perfect day at sea—whether you're an experienced diver eager to explore world-class dive sites or a casual snorkeler looking to float above vibrant coral gardens.

Nusa Penida's waters are renowned for their extraordinary clarity and the dramatic underwater topography that creates a perfect habitat for an astonishing variety of marine life. Drift along magnificent coral walls that plunge into the deep blue, explore intricate coral gardens teeming with tropical fish, and encounter majestic marine creatures including graceful manta rays, curious reef sharks, and if you're lucky, the rare ocean sunfish (Mola Mola) that frequent these waters between July and October. With visibility often exceeding 30 meters, every moment underwater becomes a breathtaking panorama of color and movement.

Our private charter experience begins with convenient pickup from your accommodation and transportation to our departure point. Once aboard your dedicated vessel, our professional crew—including certified dive instructors and marine guides—will ensure your comfort and safety while sharing their extensive knowledge of Nusa Penida's unique marine ecosystem. Between diving or snorkeling sessions, relax on deck with refreshments and a delicious lunch prepared with local ingredients. Whether you're celebrating a special occasion, seeking adventure with friends, or simply wanting the luxury of a personalized itinerary, our private charter offers the perfect blend of exclusivity, flexibility, and unforgettable underwater experiences in one of Bali's most pristine marine environments.
