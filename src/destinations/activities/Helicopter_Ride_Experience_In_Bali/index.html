Choose from a range of scenic flight paths tailored to showcase Bali's diverse beauty. Soar along the southern coastline, marveling at the limestone cliffs of Uluwatu, the pristine white sands of Pandawa and Melasti beaches, and the vibrant coral reefs below. Fly over the iconic Garuda Wisnu Kencana statue and see the bustling beaches of Kuta and Seminyak from a unique angle.

Venture further across the Badung Strait to the mystical Nusa Islands (Lembongan, Ceningan, Penida), admiring their rugged cliffs, secluded coves, and perhaps even spotting majestic Manta Rays in the clear waters. Or, embark on an epic volcano-temple journey, circling the caldera of Mount Batur, gazing upon Kintamani Lake, flying over the vast Jatiluwih Rice Terraces (UNESCO site), and witnessing the legendary Tanah Lot temple from the air. Whichever tour you choose, prepare for awe-inspiring views and memories to cherish.
