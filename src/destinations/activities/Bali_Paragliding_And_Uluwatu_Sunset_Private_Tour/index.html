Soar & Sunset: Bali Paragliding & Uluwatu Tour

Experience the thrill of tandem paragliding over stunning cliffs, visit Pandawa Beach, and witness the magical Uluwatu sunset.

Fly High, End with Magic

Locations

Timbis Beach

Duration

Best Experience

Afternoon for flight & sunset

Prepare for a day blending pure adrenaline with serene beauty on this exclusive private tour. Feel the ultimate freedom as you take to the skies on a tandem paragliding adventure! Launching from the stunning cliffs of Bali's southern peninsula (typically Timbis Beach), you'll soar like an eagle, harnessed securely with a professional instructor. Drink in the magnificent, panoramic views of turquoise waters, hidden beaches, and dramatic coastline from a perspective few ever witness. It's an exhilarating experience you'll never forget!

After your flight, unwind on the pristine white sands of the famous Pandawa Beach, often called Bali's 'Secret Beach'. Enjoy the beautiful scenery and perhaps take a refreshing dip in the ocean.

As the day draws to a close, we'll escort you to the iconic Uluwatu Temple, perched dramatically on a cliff edge 70 meters above the crashing waves. Explore this sacred sea temple, home to mischievous monkeys, and find the perfect spot to witness one of Bali's most breathtaking sunsets. Watch as the sky transforms into a fiery masterpiece, casting a golden glow over the temple and the vast Indian Ocean – a truly magical end to an adventurous day. This worry-free private tour includes comfortable hotel transfers.
