Get ready for an unforgettable dose of adrenaline mixed with breathtaking scenery in the heart of Ubud! Choose your adventure: feel the wind rush past as you zoom across lush valleys on an exhilarating zipline, or challenge your balance and pedal your way across the sky on the unique Sky Bike. Both activities offer unparalleled views of Bali's iconic rice terraces, providing a thrilling perspective you won't find anywhere else.

Safety is our top priority. Whether you're a seasoned thrill-seeker or a first-timer, our high-quality safety equipment and experienced guides ensure a secure and enjoyable experience. Feel confident as you launch off the platform or start pedaling high above the ground, knowing you're in good hands. It's the perfect way to add a memorable and fun twist to your Ubud exploration.

Want to make a full day of it? Combine your Zipline or Sky Bike adventure with an optional Ubud tour package! Explore famous landmarks like the stunning Tegalalang Rice Terrace (from a different angle!), interact with playful primates at the Ubud Monkey Forest, and cool off at the beautiful Tegenungan Waterfall. It's the ultimate Ubud experience, blending adventure, nature, and culture.
