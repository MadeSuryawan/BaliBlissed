Dive the Legendary USS Liberty Wreck in Tulamben

Explore one of the world's top wreck dive sites! Suitable for all levels, discover vibrant marine life on this unforgettable day trip.

Tulamben's Underwater Treasure

Location

Tulamben

Dive Type

Wreck Dive (Shore Entry)

Best For

All Diver Levels (Beginner to Advanced)

Embark on an extraordinary underwater journey to Tulamben, a quaint village on Bali's northeast coast, renowned globally for housing one of the most accessible and captivating wreck dives: the USS Liberty. Torpedoed in World War II, this impressive cargo ship rests just meters from the shore in shallow waters (5-30m), making it easily reachable even for snorkelers who can glimpse its outline from the surface!

This day trip offers an incredible opportunity for divers of all abilities. If you're new to diving, our professional PADI instructors will guide you in small groups (max 2 divers per instructor), ensuring a safe and comfortable introduction as you explore the shallower sections of the wreck, now encrusted with vibrant corals and teeming with colorful fish. It's a breathtaking first glimpse into the underwater world.

For certified divers, our experienced PADI Dive Masters lead small groups (max 4 divers) to delve deeper into the wreck's structure. Explore cargo holds, swim alongside massive bumphead parrotfish, and keep an eye out for larger pelagics like black-tip reef sharks or even the elusive mola mola (sunfish) and whale sharks during the right season. Advanced divers may even have the chance for thrilling swim-throughs within the wreck's interior. With typically excellent visibility and an abundance of marine biodiversity, the USS Liberty promises an unforgettable dive experience, expertly managed by one of Bali's most reputable dive operators.
