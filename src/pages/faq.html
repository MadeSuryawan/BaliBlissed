Frequently Asked Questions

General Information

What services does BaliBlissed offer?

BaliBlissed offers a comprehensive range of travel services in Bali, including:

  • Private tours and sightseeing
  • Car charter services with professional drivers
  • Adventure activities (Mount Batur sunrise, ATV rides, rafting)
  • Cultural experiences and temple tours
  • Snorkeling and diving trips
  • Custom itinerary planning

Are you a local Balinese company?

Yes! BaliBlissed is a locally-owned and operated travel company based in Bali. Our team consists of local Balinese guides and drivers who have extensive knowledge of the island's culture, hidden gems, and best experiences.

Do you provide services in English?

Absolutely! All our guides and staff speak fluent English. We also have team members who speak other languages including Indonesian, Japanese, and basic Mandarin.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods including:

Payment details will be provided upon booking confirmation.

Do I need to pay a deposit?

For most tours, we require a 30% deposit to secure your booking, with the balance due before the tour starts. For car charter services, full payment is typically required in advance.

Tours and Activities

What's included in your tour packages?

Our tour packages typically include:

  • Professional English-speaking guide
  • Private transportation with air conditioning
  • Entrance fees to attractions
  • Bottled water
  • Hotel pickup and drop-off (within certain areas)

Meals and personal expenses are usually not included unless specifically mentioned.

Can you customize tours according to my preferences?

Absolutely! We specialize in creating custom itineraries based on your interests, budget, and time constraints. Just let us know what you'd like to see and do, and we'll create a personalized experience for you.

What should I bring for the tours?

We recommend bringing:

  • Comfortable walking shoes
  • Sunscreen and hat
  • Camera
  • Light jacket for mountain tours
  • Swimwear for water activities
  • Cash for personal expenses

Specific requirements will be provided when you book.
